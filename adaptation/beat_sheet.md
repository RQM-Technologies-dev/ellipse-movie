## Emotional spine

1. Aaron wants freedom.
2. Aura arrives as the impossible answer to that want.
3. Aaron fears Aura may only be his own desire made visible.
4. Aura proves that imagination is not false; it is a creative force.
5. Eternity reveals that Aaron's love and future are part of a larger causal loop.
6. Aaron chooses trust and steps into the Portara.
