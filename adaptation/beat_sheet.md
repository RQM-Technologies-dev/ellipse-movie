## Emotional spine

1. Aaron wants freedom from the limits of Tara.
2. Aura arrives as the impossible answer to that want.
3. Aaron seeks external answers from The Teacher.
4. The Teacher makes Aaron doubt Aura's reality.
5. Aura proves that imagination can be a creative force without being false.
6. Eternity reveals that Aura is autonomous and that Aaron's future is part of a causal loop.
7. Aaron chooses trust and steps into the Portara.
