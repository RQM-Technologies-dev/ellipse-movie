# Manuscript Cleanup Rules

Use these rules when producing the clean Act I text layer.

## Preserve

- Narrative wording and sentence order.
- Chapter/movement titles.
- Proper nouns, invented terms, and unusual cosmological language.
- Intentional line breaks only when they mark structural breaks.

## Remove or normalize

- Standalone page numbers.
- Front-matter pagination inside content.
- PDF line wrapping that breaks normal paragraphs.
- Stray extraction marks that are not part of the source prose.
- Duplicate spaces introduced by conversion.

## Do not silently correct

- Spelling variants.
- Grammar issues.
- Ambiguous invented terms.
- Dialogue wording.
- Terms such as `Einai`, `Ousia`, `Portara`, and the Elgia phrase.

If a correction seems necessary for later adaptation, record it in `cleanup_log.md` and keep the raw source available for comparison.
