About the Author 
John Van Geem is an American Composer, Veteran, 
and Writer. As an accomplished pianist, John’s capacity for 
music led him to the Chicago College of Performing Arts, 
where he received his B.M. in Music Composition. With 
four published albums and hundreds of scores, John’s 
affinity for deep harmonies and unforgettable melodies 
resonate with audiences all over the world. 
Ellipse is John’s first novel and acts as the narrative 
component to the orchestral ballet of the same name. 
Recorded by the Northwest Florida Symphony Orchestra in 
2018, the orchestral score has since been published under 
“John Van Geem’s Ellipse” everywhere digital music is 
sold.   
Lateral to his accomplishments in the Arts, John 
graduated with a M.A.S. in Space Studies from Embry-
Riddle Aeronautical University, and is an avid connoisseur 
of all things aerospace. John currently resides in 
Washington, D.C. where he continues to compose.
