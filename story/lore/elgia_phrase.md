# Elgia Phrase — `Elgia lore lasant, enta ty`

## Motif

The phrase appears in several linked contexts:

1. Aura whispers a version of it when she emerges from the river.
2. Aaron sees it on the Captain's door aboard the Elgia.
3. The Captain translates it as: **The horizon of your love has no end.**
4. Aaron later finds the phrase on a plaque in the Garden of Time.
5. Eternity uses the translated line at the Portara when he calls Aaron Dad.

## Production guidance

Use the phrase sparingly. It should feel mysterious at first, lightly comic when the Captain translates it, then emotionally devastating at the Portara.

## Continuity rule

Keep spelling and capitalization consistent once a production spelling is locked.
