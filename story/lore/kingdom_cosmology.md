# Kingdom Cosmology

## Core idea

The Kingdom at the Beginning and End of the Universe exists at the focal balance where the universe's expansion moves toward its own beginning. Time does not behave as a uniform river there; it branches from the Kingdom and can be observed from multiple positions.

## Act I purpose

The Kingdom reframes the story from a rural fantasy adventure into a cosmic origin loop. It reveals that Aaron's choices are not incidental; they are foundational to the future Eternity already knows.

## Production guidance

- The Kingdom should feel ordered, musical, and impossible.
- Avoid generic heaven/space-palace imagery.
- Amethyst architecture, star reflections, impossible rooms, and temporal geometry are core visual anchors.
- The Garden of Time should feel warmer and more alive than the palace halls.
