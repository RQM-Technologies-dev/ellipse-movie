# The Portara

## Definition

The Portara is a towering Pezzotaite arch housed in the Kingdom. It sends Aaron back to his world, but not back to his past. It sends him somewhere he can start from the beginning.

## Act I function

The Portara is the Act I threshold. Aaron enters it after receiving his mission from Eternity, learning that Aura will accompany him, and hearing Eternity call him Dad.

## Production guidance

- The Portara should not look like a simple doorway.
- It should feel ancient, geometric, organic, and unavoidable.
- The audience should understand the emotional stakes before understanding the mechanics.
- The Act I cut point is Aaron walking through the gate.

## Continuity rule

The Portara is the bridge into Act II and should not be used earlier as a casual travel device.
