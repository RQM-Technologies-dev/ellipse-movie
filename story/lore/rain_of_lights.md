# The Rain of Lights

## Function

The rain of lights is the Act I inciting event. It is first experienced as terror: shockwaves, falling color, disorientation, and physical injury. Later, when Aura creates the cottage, Aaron recognizes the same visual language and realizes that the rain of lights was Aura's creative force.

## Visual vocabulary

- Iron
- Magnesium
- Chromium
- Aluminum
- Turquoise
- Silver
- Ammolite
- Azure
- Rhodonite
- Gold
- Blue/silver vapor

## Production guidance

The rain should be frightening before it becomes meaningful. Do not make it look benign in the Tara sequence. The cottage sequence can reuse the same elements with a different emotional charge: creation instead of bombardment.
